Wayonaa

the world of e-bikes one bike at a time

At Wayonaa, we are more than just a company that makes e-bikes. We are a team of passionate individuals dedicated to revolutionising the world of commute. Our commitment to innovation and quality has allowed us to create high-performance e-bikes that are a testament to our dedication.

Our philosophy is that these bikes are more than just a means of transportation; they represent a lifestyle choice that advocates for environmental sustainability and improved well-being. Therefore, our products are designed with precision and care, embodying the tenets of our brand: aggressiveness and solidity. Our passion for the work we do is reflected in every product we create, ensuring that they are all capable of delivering optimal performance.

where it all began?

Wayonaa, founded in August 2021, is a privately held company based in Surat, Gujarat. Initially focusing on imported electric vehicles, the company quickly gained market acceptance.

Building on this success, Wayonaa expanded its sales network across different regions of the state, selling over 1200 units to date.

Before entering the electric vehicle sector, the core team had significant experience in the Solar rooftop business, generating an impressive annual turnover of over INR 14 Cr.
